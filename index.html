Outcome

What functionalities that you IT system/product have?

  • Search game genre based on interest, age group, game duration, and gender
  • Popularize and constantly update games that are at the top of game charts in the world
  • Display the results of the best games by topic as quickly as possible.

How can it support to solve a real life problem?

  • This website was created with the purpose of helping those who use it to save time searching as well as thinking about which game is right for them. Along with that, the game is gradually being used by many users, regardless of whether they are young or old, they are gradually approaching the game. A website is therefore an essential tool for everyone. As for us - the members of this project team also have a purpose when designing this website based on artificial intelligence (AI) technology. The reason is that we are all interested in this type of technology, and want to learn and access it more. And this is our chance to do just that. From the above reasons, we see that this is a project that directly contributes to helping each team member have more knowledge and skills in the future learning and working process.

Details on how these functionalities work?

  1. The first page is "Home" - This is a summary of the items displayed on the site and the buttons that lead directly to those items when the user clicks.
  2. The next page is "Find Your Game" - This is where our AI program will read and remember the user's answers and display appropriate results based on those answers
  3. The third page is "Types of Game" - This page mainly displays game genres, which makes it easy for users to choose games according to their own preferences.
  4. The fourth page is "Top Chart" - This page is used to summarize and update the best games on the game charts by genre, giving users more options in finding games.
  5. The last page is "Support" - Here we will put the information of the whole group such as Contact, Address, Opening Hours, Phone number, and Location. Moreover, on this page, we also put an empty blank for users to place common questions. After the user "Published" the question, our AI program will receive and give the corresponding answers to that question. For example, "How to use this site?".

Can we use it right away? Is it complete or still very basic/early stage?

  • Because our organization hasn't acquired enough information to create this website, we can't utilize it right immediately. We only have a rudimentary understanding of the front end, and since this website also necessitates in-depth knowledge of the back end, AI, and machine learning, it is still very early in the process and the result is merely a header page.

What are the limitations and Known bugs of the system?

  • Our output is a landing page, which we believe is the greatest option based on our abilities and desire. We discovered two system constraints after creating the prototype procedure. First, we could not apply the AI system to the website due to a lack of expertise in AI, and the second constraint was regarding the database, which is an impossible undertaking because we had only learned the front end. Our result is still simple; there are superficial viewing options for visitors, and therefore no known bugs have been discovered. In contrast, when we develop an actual website based on this concept, issues may occur when more complicated features are introduced at this early stage, we just try to anticipate any bugs.

What do you plan to improve it etc?

Landscape

There are numerous of websites that help users to find games but there were only a few websites that recommended users with games which is suitable for their enthusiasm and daily routine, but none of which were applying AI technology. I had spent hours on researching game webistes and I divided those into 3 main types:

  • Game review and news website: These websites provide game reviews, previews, and news. They frequently have a huge editorial staff that plays and writes about a diverse range of games. IGN, GameSpot, and Polygon are a few examples.
  • Game distribution platforms: Users may buy and download games from these websites. They may also include elements like forums, social networking, and achievements. Steam, GOG, and the Epic Games Store are a few examples.
  • Game discovery platforms: These websites assist consumers in discovering new games that match their hobbies or tastes. They may propose games based on a user's play history or ratings, or they may allow users to explore games by genre, topic, or other criteria. itch.io and Humble Bundle are two examples.

Our team had dicuss and together decided to choose which features of those websites are necessary for our website. We tried to apply some similar functions such as game recommendations, page to select types of game, forums,… However, the most special and unique feature that is only in our website is game recommendations by AI technology, which help users exploring the suitable games for them more flexibly and accurately.

Detailed Description:

Homepage
We built all the features of the homepage that we had described in the scope section, including “Home”, “Find your games”, “Types of games”, “Top Charts”, and “Support”. When users scroll down the homepage, they will see a short description of each feature, which helps them to understand it more clearly.
Question about users' informaton and interests
We have applied some example questions to our prototype website, which help us to analyze and define the personality and daily habits of the customers. We changed our form of a question from multiple choice questions to normal-typing questions so that we might receive more detailed and precise answers from users and develop our recommendation system to be more accurate.
Game's topic pages
We also completed all of the work for the 'Types Of Games' section, as well as the list of games by category. There are several gaming genres, including action, adventure, strategy, sport, and so on. Each game category will show some suggested games, and each game will include a brief description beneath its image.
Top Charts:
On the “Top Charts” page, users can discover the leaderboards of many game types. For example, “League of Legends” is the game have the most users are playing and also receive the highest rating from the game community in the action game. The second one is “God of War: Ragnarok” and the third one is GTA V. There are also many other game types that users can explore.
Recommendations by AI system:
We could not apply AI technology due to the lack of time available to research deeper about AI technology or machine learning. To be honest, we have no experience in this area. Most of the time we had used in this project was studying how to code HTML, CSS, and Javascript. We tried our best to design an example of how our users will be suggested a suitable game by our system. This page includes our suggested game, the percentage of how it suits our users, the rating, the developer, a brief description, and a short tutorial video of that game as a background. If the users want to know more details about that game, they can click on the “Find Out More” button. They can also click on the “Next Game” button if they want to explore a new game.
Users’ feedbacks
If users want to send us any feedback or have some problems while they are using our service, they can click on the “Support” buttons at the top right of the webpage. On this page, they can send us a message about their feedback or something they would like us to improve. There are also some contact details at the bottom of the web page so that they can directly send us emails or phone messages if they want.
Game's detail pages
Each game will have its detailed description page. This page will contain the game rating, restricted age, the game’s capacity, and a more detailed description. If users want to download that game, they should click on the “More Information” button.
